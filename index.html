Time Series Forecasting Portfolio

Exploring and Comparing Key Forecasting Techniques

Welcome & Overview

Time flows relentlessly, leaving a trail of data rich with patterns, trends, and hidden signals. But deciphering these historical echoes to predict what comes next is a nuanced challenge. This portfolio isn't just a collection of models; it's an **exploration into the art and science of forecasting time series**, where I put three distinct methodologies to the test on a real dataset.

Methods Explored:

  • SARIMA (Seasonal Autoregressive Integrated Moving Average)
  • Prophet (Facebook Prophet)
  • XGBoost (Application for Time Series)

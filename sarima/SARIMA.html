SARIMA Method

SARIMA (Seasonal Autoregressive Integrated Moving Average) is a classical statistical model that extends the ARIMA model to explicitly account for seasonality in time series data. It is well-suited for data with clearly defined seasonal patterns. The model is specified by parameters (p, d, q)(P, D, Q, S), where (p, d, q) relate to the non-seasonal components and (P, D, Q, S) to the seasonal components.

Data Used

For this SARIMA demonstration, the time series data used is based on monthly data. The dataset covers the period from 2014 to 2017.
